Need assistance? Find answers to common questions below or contact us for support.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I start a new quiz?
    Go to Categories, pick your desired topic, and click Start Quiz.
  • How do I check my quiz history?
    Click History from your dashboard to see your past attempts.
  • How do I leave feedback?
    After each quiz, look for the feedback form or "Helpful/Not Helpful" buttons under explanations.
  • What if I forgot my password?
    Use the Forgot password option on the login page to reset it.
